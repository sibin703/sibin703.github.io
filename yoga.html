Yoga

  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II):
    This pose strengthens the legs, hips, and core, while also stretching the groins and chest.


  • Tree Pose (Vrksasana):
    This balancing pose strengthens the legs and core, while also improving focus and concentration.


  • Bridge Pose (Setu Bandha Sarvangasana):
    This pose strengthens the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back, while also opening the chest and improving spinal flexibility.


  • Triangle Pose (Trikonasana):
    This pose strengthens the legs and core, while also stretching the hamstrings, hips, and spine.


  • Child's Pose (Balasana):
    This gentle pose stretches the hips, thighs, and ankles, while also promoting relaxation and stress relief.


  • Plank Pose (Phalakasana):
    This pose strengthens the arms, shoulders, and core, while also improving overall posture and stability.


  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana):
    This pose strengthens the spine, while also stretching the chest, shoulders, and abdominals.
