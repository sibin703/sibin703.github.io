Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Skewers

Description

This recipe is a great way to incorporate more vegetables and lean protein into your diet. The marinade adds flavor without adding extra calories, making it a healthy and delicious meal option.

Ingredients/Steps

  • Ingredient 1: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • Ingredient 2: 1 zucchini, cut into rounds
  • ingredient 3: 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • ingredient 4: 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • ingredient 5: 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • ingredient 6: 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ingredient 7: 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • ingredient 8: 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ingredient 9: 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ingredient 10: Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Step 1: Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2: Thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers, alternating the chicken and vegetables..
  3. Step 3: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.
  4. Step 4: Brush the marinade generously over the chicken and vegetables.
  5. step 5: Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Step 6: Serve the skewers hot and enjoy!

roasted sweet potato and quinoa salad

Description

This salad is packed with nutrients from the sweet potato, quinoa, spinach, and pumpkin seeds. It's also easy to prepare and makes for a satisfying and delicious meal.

Ingredients/Steps

  • Ingredient 1: 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • Ingredient 2: 1 cup quinoa
  • ingredient 3: 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • ingredient 4: 2 cups baby spinach
  • ingredient 5: 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • ingredient 6: 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • ingredient 7: 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ingredient 8: 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • ingredient 9: 1 teaspoon honey
  • ingredient 10: Salt and pepper, to taste
  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Step 2: Spread the cubed sweet potato on a baking sheet, drizzle with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender and lightly browned.
  3. Step 3: In a saucepan, bring the quinoa and vegetable broth (or water) to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.
  4. Step 4: In a small bowl, whisk together the remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. step 5: In a large bowl, combine the roasted sweet potato, cooked quinoa, baby spinach, and red onion. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  6. Step 6: Sprinkle pumpkin seeds on top and serve immediately.

Blueberry Oatmeal Breakfast Bowl

Description

This breakfast bowl is packed with fiber, protein, and healthy fats from the oats, almonds, and blueberries. It's also naturally sweetened with honey and vanilla extract, making it a delicious and nutritious way to start your day.

Ingredients/Steps

  • Ingredient 1: 1 cup rolled oats
  • Ingredient 2: 2 cups almond milk (or milk of your choice)
  • ingredient 3: 1 tablespoon honey
  • ingredient 4: 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ingredient 5: 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • ingredient 6: 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • ingredient 7: 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • ingredient 8: Pinch of salt
  1. Step 1: In a medium-sized saucepan, combine the rolled oats, almond milk, honey, vanilla extract, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring occasionally.
  2. Step 2: Reduce the heat to low and let the oatmeal simmer for about 5 minutes or until it reaches your desired consistency. Stir occasionally to prevent the oatmeal from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  3. Step 3: Once the oatmeal is cooked, pour it into a bowl.
  4. Step 4: Top the oatmeal with fresh blueberries and sliced almonds.
  5. step 5: Drizzle a little extra honey on top if desired.
  6. Step 6: Enjoy your delicious and healthy breakfast bowl!
